Integration Guide

Why retailers need Lightspeed X (Vend) → Amazon integration

Managing both in-store and Amazon sales creates constant inventory movement. A product sold in a store must reduce the Amazon quantity immediately — and vice versa. Without automation, retailers face overselling, order cancellations, negative reviews, and Buy Box instability. Manual updates of prices, quantities and listings are slow and error-prone. That’s why a robust Vend-to-Amazon real-time inventory integration is essential for retailers who want to scale both channels simultaneously.

SKUPlugs is middleware built to connect Lightspeed X (formerly Vend) and Amazon Seller Central, automating product syncs, inventory updates, and order flows so your offline and online operations run as one.

Lightspeed X and Amazon Integration by SKUPlugs

How SKUPlugs enables smooth Lightspeed X and Amazon integration

1. Real-time inventory sync

SKUPlugs provides powerful two-way syncing. When you sell in-store, stock levels update on Amazon immediately. When an Amazon sale occurs, Lightspeed X reflects the change instantly. This prevents overselling, improves seller metrics, and keeps availability accurate across channels.

2. Automated order sync

Amazon orders automatically appear inside your Lightspeed X POS with customer, order and item details. This removes manual entry, reduces errors, and speeds fulfillment — saving hours of daily work for store staff.

3. Simplified product and listing automation

If your product catalog already lives in Lightspeed X, SKUPlugs can push titles, descriptions, variants, pricing, and images directly to Amazon. This consolidated listing workflow means you don’t manage listings separately in Seller Central.

4. Multichannel flexibility

SKUPlugs supports selling across Amazon marketplaces (US, Canada, UK, EU, Australia and more) and can handle multiple Amazon accounts or regions while keeping Lightspeed X synchronized.
